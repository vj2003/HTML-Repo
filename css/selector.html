Honda City

The Honda City is a subcompact car which has been produced by the Japanese manufacturer Honda since 1981. The City was originally a 3-door hatchback/2-door convertible for the Japanese, European and Australasian markets.The 3-door City was retired in 1994 after the second-generation and replaced by the Logo. This generation also marks the introduction of the 5-door hatchback model starting from 2020.
