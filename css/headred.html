THAR

The Thar was first introduced in 2010 as a modernized version of the Mahindra Legend, which was based on the Mahindra MM540, a vehicle that was in production in India since the 1980s.
The Mahindra Thar was designed to be a rugged, reliable, and affordable off-road vehicle that could handle the rough terrain found in many parts of India. The Thar's design is based on the iconic Jeep CJ series, which Mahindra had been producing under license since the 1940s.
